Battlewand Fantasy

“In the blinding brightness of the near future, it should be peaceful... and yet it's not.”

Battle Wand Fantasy is a tabletop wargame where players create a team of magical warriors and battle on a hex grid using a limited amount of points to allocate toughness and special abilities. It's a strategic game that promises an exciting and thrilling experience.
